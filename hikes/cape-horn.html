Cape Horn Lookout

The Cape Horn Trail is a new trail and one of the closest Gorge trails to the Portland-Vancouver metropolitan area. It features gorgeous views, rocky crags, streams and two waterfalls. The trail is very well signed. There are both metal stakes and wooden signs at each junction.

Plan Your Trip

    Trail Aspects:
  • Exertion: Moderate, 8 Miles Round-Trip
  • Sights: Views of the Columbia River and Multnomah Falls, Waterfalls
  • Popularity: July - January
  • Open: All Year
    Directions From Portland:
  • Travel Time: Approximately 30 minutes (31 miles from downtown)
  • Take I-205 into Washington and onto Highway 14 E
  • At milepost 25, look for the parking lot that marks the trailhead
