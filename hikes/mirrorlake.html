Mirror Lake

The Mirror Lake trail has the misfortune of being one of the few hikes accessible directly from Highway 26, and thus suffers a crush of visitors on weekends. The attention is well-deserved: the always well-graded trail takes you past a picture-postcard views of Mount Hood and rugged Tom Dick and Harry Mountain, towering above Mirror Lake.

Plan Your Trip

    Trail Aspects:
  • Exertion: Easy, 3 miles round trip
  • Sights: Beautiful views of Mt. Hood and Tom Dick and Harry Mountain reflected upon Mirror Lake.
  • Popularity: High
  • Open: All Year
    Directions From Portland:
  • Travel Time: approximately 90 minutes (54 Miles from Portland)
  • Follow I-84 E/U.S. 30 E and I-205 S to Clackamas County. Take exit 14 from I-205 S
  • Get on US-26 E/Mt Hood Hwy in Boring from SE Sunnyside Rd and OR-212 E/SE Hwy 212
  • Merge onto US-26 E/Mt Hood Hwy Destination will be on the right
