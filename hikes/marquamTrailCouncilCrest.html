Marquam Trail Council Crest Hike

Easily one of Portland's best close-in hikes, the Marquam Trail is as pleasant as any in Forest Park and features an outstanding four-mountain view from Council Crest Park, once the site of a popular amusement park. The hike has about 1100 feet of total elevation gain, so it's perfect for anyone looking for a good workout without expending the time or gas to reach hikes farther afield. It's also a perfect winter hike for those rainy, foggy days when you can't buy a view in the Columbia Gorge.

Plan Your Trip

    Trail Aspects:
  • Exertion: Moderate, 5.8 Miles Round-Trip
  • Sights: Wildlife, Old Growth, Views of 4 different mountains.
  • Popularity: Low
  • Open: All Year
    Directions From Portland:
  • Travel Time: Approximately 10 minutes (4.7 Miles from center Portland)
  • Follow Ross Island Bridge and Pacific Hwy W to SW Hamilton St
  • Continue on SW Hamilton St. Drive to SW Terwilliger Blvd.
