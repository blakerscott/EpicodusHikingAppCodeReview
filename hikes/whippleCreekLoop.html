Whipple Creek Loop

Whipple Creek protects a 300-acre stand of native forest in the north Vancouver area just west of the Clark County Fairgrounds. There are over five miles of trails here, going up and down through shallow gullies, and a good loop can be made around the perimeter. The remains of an old grist mill are one of the highlights. The park has a meadow with a picnic table, but trails can be very muddy in the wet season. Be prepared to encounter horses.
