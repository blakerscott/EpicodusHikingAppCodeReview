Lower Macleay Park to Pittock Mansion

Macleay Park feels like Rivendale from Lord of the Rings. The trail runs alongside a creek, where everything is wet and green and alive. Several immense trees that fell decades-to-centuries ago are wedged above the creek. This trail is GORGEOUS. It connects to the Wildwood trail, which can be taken up to Pittock Mansion and even as far out as Hoyt Arboretum if you really want to go for it.

Plan Your Trip

    Trail Aspects:
  • Exertion: Moderate, 5 Miles Round-Trip
  • Sights: Views of Downtown Portland
  • Popularity: Moderate
  • Open: All Year
    Ammenities at the Mansion:
  • Bathrooms
  • Gift Shop
  • Bus line: 15
  • Tour the Historic Pittock Mansion
    Directions From Portland:
  • Travel Time: Approximately 10 minutes from downtown
  • Get on I-5 S to 405 S (Fremont Bridge)
  • Take the Highway 30 W exit towards St.Helens, exit immediately onto Vaughn St.
  • Turn Left at the first light on NW 23rd, right onto Thurman Ave, right onto NW 28th, left onto Upshur.
